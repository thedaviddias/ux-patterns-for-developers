## Overview

UX Patterns for Devs ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## What We Collect

**We do not track our users.** We don't use analytics, cookies for tracking, or any other user monitoring tools.

### Newsletter

If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is the only personal information we store.

- Your email is used solely to send you newsletter updates
- We never share or sell your email to third parties
- You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any newsletter email
